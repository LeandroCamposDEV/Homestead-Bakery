About Us

Welcome to Homestead Bakery, a slice of London tradition in every bite. Since 1924, our family-owned bakery has been serving the local community with handcrafted breads, sweets, and cakes. Born in the heart of London, Homestead Bakery stands as a testament to the love and dedication to classic recipes passed down through generations. We use only the finest local ingredients to ensure unparalleled quality and freshness. Here, every product not only nourishes the body but also celebrates our rich cultural heritage. Come discover the flavors that make Homestead Bakery a landmark in London baking.

Our Space

At Homestead Bakery, we've created an environment where every visit is a memorable experience. Our space is designed to welcome everyone who appreciates fine food in a charming and cozy setting. Blending rustic and modern decor, we offer the perfect backdrop for special moments together. Whether it's for a romantic breakfast, an afternoon of sweets and tea, or a peaceful evening, our nooks are ideal for couples looking to enjoy some time together. Additionally, our prime location in London adds a touch of magic and history to your visit. Come and experience this at Homestead Bakery, where every detail is thoughtfully curated for your happiness and comfort.

Baking course

Preparing the Dough

Discover the joy of making bread from scratch! Our introductory course teaches basic dough preparation techniques, allowing you to feel the perfect texture beneath your hands. Ideal for beginners passionate about the art of baking.

Baking the Breads

Experience the enticing aroma of fresh bread coming out of the oven! In this module of the course, you will learn how to bake breads to perfection, understanding the ideal timing and temperature for each type of recipe.

Ready Breads

Admire your creation! At the end of the course, you will have the opportunity to see and taste the breads you've prepared. Celebrate your new skill with delicious breads, ready to be shared with friends and family.
